about us

OurStore story begins in Amaan, Jordan in the 1940’s. Wessam found a recipe in the local newspaper that would inspire his “Original” Cheesecake. Everyone loved his recipe so much that he decided to open a small Cheesecake shop, but he eventually gave up his dream of owning his own business in order to raise his two small children, he moved his baking equipment to a kitchen in his basement and continued to supply cakes to several of the best restaurants in town while raising his family.!
